Welcome to Word Search

Here's How to Play!

  1. Choose Your Device
    • On the Home Screen, select whether you are playing on a computer or a mobile device by clicking the corresponding button.
  2. Understand the Objective
    • Your goal is to find all the hidden words listed in the puzzle.
    • Words can be arranged horizontally, vertically, or in a straight line.
  3. How to Select Words
    • On a Touchscreen (Mobile/Tablet):
      • Use your finger to drag across the letters of a word.
    • On a computer:
      • Click and drag your mouse across the letters of a word
        • Once you've selected a word correctly
      • The word will be highlighted.
      • It will be marked as "found" on your list
  4. Use Hints
    • If you get stuck:
      • Look at the hint box for clues about the words you're trying to find.
      • Keep an eye on the additional hints that appear after a few attempts
  5. Complete All Words
    • Once all the words are found or marked as "failed," a Final Question will appear.
    • Answer the Final Question to complete the game.
  6. Answer the Final Question
    • Type your answer into the input box provided
    • Click "Submit" to see if you're correct
      • You will have multiple attempts to answer correctly
      • If you run out of attempts, the correct answer will be revealed
